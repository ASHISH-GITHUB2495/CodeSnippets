Bibliography
Health Behavior in School-Aged Children (HBSC), 2009-2010

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 34792. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. This list represents all items known
to ICPSR as of 2014-09-26.

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
bibliography@icpsr.umich.edu.


1. Brooks-Russell, Ashley; Farhat, Tilda; Haynie, Denise; Simons-Morton,
Bruce, "Trends in substance use among 6th- to 10th-grade students from 1998
to 2010: Findings from a national probability study." Journal of Early
Adolescence. in press 2013,. DOI: 10.1177/0272431613501409

2. Iannotti, Ronald, J.; Jing Wang, "Trends in physical activity, sedentary
behavior, diet, and BMI among US adolescents, 2001-2009." Pediatrics. Oct
2013, 132, (4), 606 - 614. DOI: 10.1542/peds.2013-1488

3. Lipsky, Leah M.; Iannotti, Ronald J., "Associations of television
viewing with eating behaviors in the 2009 Health Behaviour in School-aged
Children Study." Archives of Pediatrics and Adolescent Medicine. May 2012,
166, (5), 465 - 472. DOI: 10.1001/archpediatrics.2011.1407
